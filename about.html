About Blossoms & The School of Sciences Journey

Blossoms is the intra-deanery cultural fest at Christ (Deemed To Be University) Bangalore with the motto 'Be the best'. This is a unique venture where all 7000 students of Christ University, Bangalore participate in a cultural event. The events are divided into four categories - Art, Theatre, Stage & Literary. Students compete in multiple events across these categories. The winners lay their hands on the Inter-Deanery trophy for Artistic Excellence and Service.

The Journey Through Blossoms

Intra-Deanery Prelims

The initial round sees fierce but friendly competition within individual deaneries, identifying the brightest stars and strongest contenders.

Inter-Deanery Finals

The champions from each deanery then advance to the grand stage, where they compete at an inter-deanery level, vying for ultimate glory and the coveted Blossoms title.

Representing the School of Sciences: Team Onyx

As part of the vibrant tapestry of Blossoms, the School of Sciences proudly fields its own dedicated team. We are Team Onyx, embodying the spirit of innovation, precision, and boundless creativity inherent to the sciences.

Team Color

White – symbolizing clarity, brilliance, and the unity that empowers us.

Team Name

Onyx – representing strength, resilience, and the polished excellence we bring to every challenge.

Team Slogan

"From flames to flights, White Onyx Ignites." – A testament to our journey, transforming raw passion and scientific insight into soaring artistic achievements that captivate and inspire.

At Blossoms, Team Onyx from the School of Sciences aims not just to participate, but to lead. We encourage all our students to embrace this incredible opportunity – to join us, participate actively across all events, and help us secure a commanding lead in the inter-deanery finals.

This is more than a competition; it's a celebration of talent, a forging of camaraderie, and a testament to the diverse capabilities within the School of Sciences. Join us in making Blossoms an unforgettable experience for Team Onyx!
